## The Problem This App Solves

Based on research into common pain points people face in 2025:

- **59% of Americans** are stressed by inflation and rising prices
- **38% of people** are stressed by unexpected expenses (medical bills, emergency repairs)
- **50% of people** struggle with high living expenses (rent, groceries, utilities)
- Only **15% of Americans** report no financial stressors

Financial stress is the #1 pain point people experience in daily life.

## Solution

Budget Guardian helps you:

1. **Track Unexpected Expenses** - Quickly log expenses as they happen, with special highlighting for unexpected costs
2. **Set Monthly Budgets** - Define your spending limit and get visual feedback on your progress
3. **Get Proactive Alerts** - Receive warnings at 75%, 90%, and 100% of your budget
4. **Visualize Spending** - See where your money goes with category-based statistics
5. **Stay in Control** - Monitor your remaining budget in real-time
