#### 1. **Mood-Based Music Discovery Engine**

#### 2. **AI-Powered Playlist Auto-Generation**
The system analyzes your complete taste DNA and generates playlists with a natural narrative arc:

- **Peak phase** (40%): Highest energy/emotion that matches your preferences

**Why it matters:** Most playlists are just random collections. This creates a journey that actually feels intentional.

#### 3. **Temporal Music Recommendations (Time-of-Day Aware)**
Different times of day call for different music:
- **5am-12pm (Morning):** High energy (60-100), high valence (50-100) to wake you up
- **12pm-5pm (Afternoon):** Balanced energy (40-80) for focus and productivity
- **5pm-9pm (Evening):** Lower energy (30-70), varied valence for relaxation
- **9pm-5am (Night):** Minimal energy (20-50) for wind-down and sleep

The system generates entirely different playlists based on when you generate them, understanding that your listening context matters.

**Why it matters:** Spotify doesn't care what time it is. Your music should adapt to your circadian rhythm and daily activities.

#### 10. **Music-to-Workout Matcher (Syncs BPM with Exercise)**
Different workouts need different musical properties:
- **Running (140-160 BPM):** Consistent high BPM for pace maintenance
- **Cycling (120-140 BPM):** Slightly lower, but energetic and driving
- **Strength Training (100-130 BPM):** Heavy beats for power lifts
- **HIIT (150-180 BPM):** Explosive, unpredictable energy
- **Yoga (60-90 BPM):** Calm, meditative, grounding

The system generates workout-optimized playlists with a three-phase arc:
1. **Warm-up:** Lower BPM to ease into exercise
2. **Peak:** Maximum BPM matching your workout type
3. **Cool-down:** Decreasing intensity to recover

**Why it matters:** Wrong BPM music actually messes with your workout. Right BPM lifts you.

**Why it matters:** Smart queuing makes listening feel effortless.

## ðŸ”§ API Endpoints

### Discovery & Mood
- `POST /api/discover/mood` - Discover by mood description
- `POST /api/discover/emotion-combination` - Find songs blending emotions
- `GET /api/discover/hidden-gems/:userId` - Find underrated gems

- `POST /api/playlists/generate` - Generate smart playlist
- `POST /api/playlists/temporal` - Time-of-day aware playlist
- `POST /api/playlists/workout` - Workout-optimized playlist
- `POST /api/playlists/life-event` - Life event matcher

### Radio
- `POST /api/radio/create` - Create smart radio station
- `GET /api/radio/:stationId/next-segment` - Get next radio segment

### Analytics
- `GET /api/analytics/listening-habits/:userId` - Complete habit analysis
- `GET /api/analytics/mood-evolution/:userId` - Mood trends over time
- `GET /api/user/:userId/taste-dna` - Music taste profile

### Exploration
- `GET /api/eras/:era/:userId` - Explore music by era
- `GET /api/social/compare/:userId/:friendId` - Compare taste with friend
- `GET /api/memories/:userId` - Get music memories timeline
